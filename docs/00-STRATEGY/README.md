## FRL Strategy Context

Florida Ramp & Lift needs an internal operational intelligence platform that turns fragmented field work, WillScot work orders, contractor documentation, invoice readiness, and payout logic into structured reviewable data.

The MVP goal is internal operational control before live integrations, client portal expansion, or autonomous automation.
