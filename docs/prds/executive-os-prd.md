# FLR Executive OS PRD

Status: Placeholder

## Problem

Executive decisions need a reliable view of operations, billing, exceptions, and follow-up without depending on scattered messages or manual summaries.

## Desired Outcome

Create a documented product surface for executive visibility and decision support that rolls into the Centralized Control Dashboard.

## Success Criteria

- Required executive metrics are defined.
- Data sources are mapped to canonical schemas.
- Human review gates are explicit.
- No live integrations are assumed.

## Scope

- Executive summaries
- Operational exceptions
- Billing visibility
- Dashboard requirements

## Out of Scope

- Automated approvals
- Live third-party integrations
- App implementation

## Constraints

- No Firebase.
- No secrets.
- No sensitive client documents.
- Existing Phase 1 docs remain authoritative until superseded by approval.

## Open Questions

- Which executive reports are required weekly?
- What exceptions should be escalated immediately?
