# PROJECT_SOURCE

## Mission

Build and maintain the operational memory and intelligence layer for Florida Ramp & Lift field operations.

## Operational context

This system supports real-world infrastructure work, including:

- accessibility installations
- ramps and lifts
- modular project support
- WillScot modular workflows
- dispatch and field service operations
- OSHA-conscious jobsite execution

## Core objective (phase 1)

Ingest WillScot/Mobile Mini service report PDFs and convert them into structured operational job records.

## Required extracted fields (minimum)

- work order number
- SAP order number
- product unit number
- client
- site address
- contact information
- line items and quantities
- job scope
- dispatch requirements
- safety requirements
- billing fields
- completion/signature status

## Build philosophy

AI augments consistency, speed, extraction quality, analysis, and repeatable workflows.

AI does **not** justify speculative architecture, SaaS bloat, or hidden abstractions.

## Priorities

1. schemas
2. documentation
3. SOPs
4. operational workflows
5. deterministic parsing
6. structured extraction
7. auditability
8. safety
9. maintainability
